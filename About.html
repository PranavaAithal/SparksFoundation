About Us


Welcome to EHF Donations!

At EHF Donations, we are committed to improving the lives of disadvantaged children by putting an emphasis on their education, health, and nutrition. Regardless of their socioeconomic status, we passionately believe that every child deserves equal opportunity and the ability to succeed.
Our goal is to empower and elevate poor children by giving them access to a top-notch education, crucial medical treatment, and wholesome food. We think that education is the key to ending the cycle of poverty and that a child's whole development is fundamentally impacted by their physical and nutritional health.




What We Do: & How You Can Help

We have been able to make a meaningful difference in the lives of numerous poor children thanks to the kind donations and support of people like you. Together, we have seen children overcome poverty, receive access to education, and have better health outcomes. We can expand our impact and achieve even more with your ongoing support.
Join us in our effort to give disadvantaged children access to food, health care, and education. Your contributions, no matter how great or small, can change lives and help create a better future. You can directly contribute to our activities by visiting our website, or you can look into volunteer opportunities. There are many benefits to donating your old stuff to EHF Donation.

  • Education: To ensure that every child has access to school, we put forth great effort. To assist kids reach their greatest potential, we sponsor educational institutions, grant scholarships, and provide instructional materials.
  • Health: We work to enhance the physical and mental well-being of disadvantaged children. To meet their medical needs, we arrange medical camps, coordinate health examinations, and offer crucial healthcare services.
  • Food: We are aware of the significance of a healthy diet for a child's development. We provide wholesome meals and work with neighbourhood groups to develop long-term approaches to food security.
